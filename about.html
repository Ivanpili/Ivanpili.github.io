My Journey

I view data through the lens of an engineer: precision, structure, and reliability are non-negotiable. My background began in Aeronautical Engineering, where the margin for error is zero. That discipline taught me how to diagnose complex systems and predict failures before they occur.

Transitioning into Information Technology and Cybersecurity, I realized that the most valuable asset in any system is the data it generates. Today, I combine that engineering rigor with modern data science tools to help organizations make safer, smarter decisions.

Whether I am optimizing patient workflows in the Healthcare sector or analyzing risk patterns in Aviation technical consulting, my goal is the same: to turn raw noise into clear, decision-ready intelligence.
